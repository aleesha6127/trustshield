📚 Safety Guidelines

  • ✔ Trust your instincts—if it feels wrong, leave immediately.
  • ✔ Share your live location with trusted contacts.
  • ✔ Carry safety tools (pepper spray, whistle, or alarm).
  • ✔ Walk with confidence and stay aware of surroundings.
  • ✔ shout "FIRE" instead of "HELP" to attract more attention.

  • ✖ Never walk alone in poorly lit or isolated areas.
  • ✖ Don't wear both headphones while walking alone.
  • ✖ Don't share OTPs or personal details with strangers.
  • ✖ Don't argue with an aggressor—prioritize escape.
  • ✖ Don't reveal you are home alone to delivery personnel.

  • 🛡 Use Two-Factor Authentication (2FA) everywhere.
  • 🛡 Be careful with geolocation tags on social media photos.
  • 🛡 Regularly check app permissions for location access.
